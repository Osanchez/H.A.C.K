H.A.C.K

Description:

When you're out drinking, do you know how much alcohol is in your body? No, you don't. Our product is very simple. You put in your weight, gender, number of hours since you've begun drinking, and the number of beers you've drunk. We put it into our algorithm, and you have your Blood Alcohol Content (BAC). In addition to that, we recommend what you can and cannot do while your BAC is at a certain level. This is available through our website or our mobile application.
